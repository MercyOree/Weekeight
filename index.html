TecSupportAcross: Where we donate unused tech supplies

About Us

Welcome to TecSupportAcross, where we believe in the transformative power of technology. Our mission is to make a positive impact by repurposing and donating old technology to create opportunities, foster education, and bridge the digital divide. We recycle donated technolgy devices like computers, refurbished them, and load them with cool tech software. At TecSupportAcross, we recognize the invaluable role that technology plays in shaping the future. However, we understand that not everyone has equal access to these opportunities. Our mission is to address this disparity by breathing new life into old technology and putting it in the hands of those who need it most.
